Spinach & Strawberry Salad

← Back to Recipes

Ingredients

  • 4 cups spinach
  • 1 cup strawberries
  • ¼ cup walnuts
  • ¼ cup feta cheese
  • Balsamic vinaigrette

Instructions

Combine all ingredients in a large bowl. Toss with dressing and serve immediately.


Why It's Good for Iron

Spinach provides non-heme iron, while strawberries are rich in vitamin C, which helps your body absorb iron more efficiently.
