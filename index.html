About Us

My name is Sriya and I am a high school student passionate about spreading womens' health information. I have been an active member of Girl Scouts, a girl-led organization dedicated to developing leadership, communication, and life skills for the past 10 years. As part of my journey, I am completing my Gold Award, the highest honor in Girl Scouts, by creating a sustainable and measurable solution to address a community issue. My project focuses on raising awareness about iron-deficiency anemia among teenage girls by promoting healthy nutrition and educating them on anemia prevention.
